# 83Plus:BCALLs:4EE5

Source: https://wikiti.brandonw.net/index.php?title=83Plus:BCALLs:4EE5

## Synopsis

**Unofficial Name:** SendAByte

**BCALL Address:** 4EE5

"Sends a byte over the link port."

## Inputs

- A: byte to transmit

## Outputs

- None

## Destroys

- All registers

## Comments

The operation will "throw ERR:LINK if any problems are found" during transmission.
